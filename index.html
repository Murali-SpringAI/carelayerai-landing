CareLayer AI

Building an AI-powered platform to improve healthcare operations, financial workflows, workflow automation, and decision support.

The Problem

Healthcare organizations continue to operate with fragmented workflows, manual coordination, disconnected data, and administrative bottlenecks. These gaps create operational inefficiency, revenue leakage, avoidable rework, and delayed decision-making.

Our Approach

CareLayer AI is being designed as a modular intelligence layer that works with existing healthcare systems instead of replacing them. The goal is to help teams automate workflows, surface insights, reduce administrative friction, and make better operational decisions.

Hospital Operations Improve visibility into workflows, escalations, delays, and operational coordination.
Revenue Cycle & Claims Reduce billing errors, missed charges, claim denials, and underpayment risk.
Prior Authorization Support documentation readiness, payer-specific rules, and faster turnaround.
Decision Support Use AI-assisted data access and semantic search to help teams answer workflow questions faster.

Current Focus

We are currently engaging with healthcare operators, clinicians, product leaders, and technology executives across the US and India to validate high-impact problem areas before building production-ready solutions.
