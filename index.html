CareLayer

Building an AI-powered platform to improve healthcare operations, financial workflows, and decision support.

The Problem

Healthcare systems continue to operate on fragmented workflows, manual coordination, and disconnected data. This leads to inefficiencies in operations, revenue leakage, administrative burden, and delayed decision-making.

Our Approach

CareLayer is designed as a modular intelligence layer that works with existing healthcare systems — enabling automation, workflow visibility, and better operational decisions without requiring organizations to replace their core infrastructure.

  • Hospital operations and workflow automation
  • Billing, claims, and revenue cycle optimization
  • Prior authorization and administrative efficiency
  • AI-assisted decision support and intelligent data access
  • Notification and coordination platforms for operational teams

Current Focus

We are currently engaging with healthcare operators, clinicians, product leaders, and technology executives to validate high-impact problem areas before building production-ready solutions.
